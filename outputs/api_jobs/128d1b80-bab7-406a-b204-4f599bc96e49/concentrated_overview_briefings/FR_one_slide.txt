**Executive Market Diagnostic: Apparel & Luxury Industry**

The market exhibits moderate purchasing power dynamics, with GDP growth stabilizing post-pandemic but constrained by inflationary pressures and labor market volatility. Tourism monetization remains a critical growth lever, with high expenditure per arrival (e.g., €859M in 2024) and strong European inbound dominance (70%+ of arrivals), though dependency risks persist given cyclical external shocks. Demographically, the population is aging, with core working-age cohorts (25–54) plateauing, while net migration (90K+ annually) and stable household sizes (2.25) mitigate long-term contraction. Structurally, the market is **premium-concentrated but cyclically sensitive**, with tourism-driven demand and a shrinking domestic consumer base. Strategic positioning should prioritize high-value tourism segments while hedging against demographic and external volatility.
